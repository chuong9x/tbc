Improving Performance by Optimising CPU Voltage

Daniel Gijsbers noticed my descriptionand solution to the of MacBook Pro overheating problem and very kindly responded:

I read your blog post. Regarding your mac book having heat problems. It’s good you cleaned the fans.

What you could also look into is whether improving performance by optimising CPU voltage is also possible for a mac. Basically, I lower the voltage to my CPU to the point it is just stable, and with that reduction, I can run the processor at a higher clock speed.

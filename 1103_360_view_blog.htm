The 360 View

There is a new kid on the block!

The 360 View is a blog providing technical resources about BIM 360 product families, including Glue and Field, including API coverage, by Manu Venugopal, Integration Expert in Autodesk Consulting and our ADN API liaison to the BIM 360 Glue product team.

Manu and Mikako Harada, ADN AEC technical lead, worked together closely on the BIM 360 Glue API. Manu is the behind the scenes 'brain' for ADN support in this area and helped a lot preparing the last two Developer Days presentations about BIM 360.

As Mikako already pointed out, the Glue API is still in a pilot stage and the number of people who can actually use it is currently very limited. We are actively discussing ways to expand the audience and I hope you will find The 360 View a good source to learn about and keep eyes on the progress of BIM 360.
